Recent Work: Ember - Break App

I decided to try to keep this project within a 4-5 hours time frame and was successful. Basic idea came from a count up timer in the ember 101 video series that sparked an idea to make this into a count down timer. While coding we all need to take breaks, just standing up and grabbing a glass of water, plus a bit of stretching can be accomplised in three minutes.

Very Specific Use Case

With the limited amount of time that I gave myself to accomplish this task, I went for my specific use case only. My need was to stop coding, to sit back, to have this big old timer staring back at me as I chilled out.
