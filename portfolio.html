Recent Work: Responsive Design - This Portfolio

In this project I showcase variations of my own portfolio's design based on the device the design is viewed on. I built this site mobile first with the idea of progressive enhancement, giving more features as the device scales in size. This is seen as you reach a tablet or desktop in size and more details appear on the front page, describing my work. On phones there are icons to scroll the page that are not on the larger screens.
