Recent Work: Responsive Design - This Portfolio

In this project I showcase variations of my own portfolio's design based on the device the design is viewed on. I built this site mobile first with the idea of progressive enhancement, giving more features as the device scales up in size. This is seen as you reach a tablet or desktop in size and more details appear on the front page, describing my work. On phones there are icons to scroll the page that are not on the larger screens.

Phone and Mobile Strategy

Giving different levels of information is important, phone vs. tablet. Phones are more often on the cellular network and the user needs information as accessible as possible. Someone on an tablet is more likely to be on wi-fi and relaxing a bit more allowing pages to load slower if needed, enjoying more content.

Computers Maximize Information

When on a desktop or laptop, users have the ability to consume larger amounts of information. This allows the content strategy to be significantly different. These devices are ones where people are less on the go, and typically sitting down. This alone changes the pace of the individual while using the internet.
